About Recyclical

Recyclical was built to aid in New York City's Zero Waste initiatives. Recyclical is an easy-to-use app that helps you find information about the resources in your neighborhood, so you can join your city in reducing the 4 million tons of waste shipped to landfills each year.

“New York City residents currently recycle only about 17% of their total waste – half of what they could be recycling under the current program.”

– GROWNYC
